ABOUT ME

I'm a computer engineering graduate from the University of Maryland. I love using my software engineering skills to build tools and applications to solve real problems. That is, if you think figuring out who the best crossword puzzle solver amongst your friends is a real problem...

Currently, I'm working on the Terragraph and Evenstar projects within the connectivity infrastructure program at Facebook. In addition to software defined networking, my technical interests include machine learning, edge computing, data infrastructure, and serverless application development.

Outside of work and side projects, I love to spend time outdoors skiing, fishing, biking, and hiking. You can also find me rooting for Liverpool F.C. or trying to spell "Kyrgyzstan" correctly at the occasional trivia night.
